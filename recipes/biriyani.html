Chicken Biriyani

Chicken Biryani: an opulent masterpiece of aromatic spices and tender, succulent chicken, layered with 
fragrant basmati rice. Each spoonful unveils a symphony of flavors– 
from the warmth of cinnamon to the zing of ginger.
Creating a culinary voyage through the heart of India's rich culinary heritage.

Ingrediaents

  • 1 whole Chicken (Cut into 8 pieces)
  • 5 whole tomatoes (chopped)
  • 6 onions Sliced (Deep fried until golden and crispy)
  • 1 chilli (Chopped)
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1–2 tbsp Cumin (ground),1 tbsp sugar,1 tsp cinnamon (ground),1/4 tsp cloves (ground),1 tsp cardamom (ground),1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp ginger (crushed)
  • 1 tsp garlic (crushed)
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 3 potatoes (peeled; halved & deep fried)
  • 3 tbsp Coriander (roughly chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Mala (or natural yoghurt)

Instructions

  1. In a tshick bottomed pan, add 1 tbsp ghee followed by the tomato paste and fry it for 1 min on medium heat.
  2. Add 1 tbsp cumin, 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon, 1 tsp ground cardamom, 1/2 tsp black pepper and a few cloves (4-5).
  3. Cook the spices for 2 min then add the chopped tomatoes and cover the pan.
  4. After 13 min, add the chicken, garlic, ginger, fresh chilly and, salt.
  5. Cover and cook until chicken soften (about 15min).
  6. Add 1/2 cup of mala or natural yoghurt cover and reduce the heat.
  7. Deep fDeep fry your sliced onions if you haven’t and also fry the potatoes until a skin forms.
  8. Add the deep friend and onions and potatoes to the biriyani. Cook until the potatoes are soft.
